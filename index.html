DIGITAL EVENTS PLACE @ THE PARC:
YOUR NEWEST VENUE UNDER THE NEW NORMAL

Globaltronics and The Performing Arts and Recreation Center (PARC) Foundation strengthened their partnership by transforming several of the latter’s spaces into a one-stop digital events place for businesses, individuals, artists, families, and others.

HEALTH AND SAFETY PROTOCOLS

With our newest digital events venue, more industries can now thrive under the new normal while assuring health and safety.

STATE-OF-THE-ART DIGITAL DISPLAYS AND AUDIOVISUAL EQUIPMENT

Entertain your virtual attendees with our vibrant and high-quality digital solutions combined with top-of-the-line audiovisual equipment.

HIGHLY SKILLED CREATIVE AND TECHNICAL PROFESSIONALS

We have professional creative artists who can transform your ideas into amazing virtual events.


We also have a skilled production technical operator who can support you in configuring, installing, and maintaining technical systems.

MULTI-PURPOSE ROOMS PERFECT FOR PRE- AND POST-PRODUCTION

Aside from the digital spaces, DigiPARC also has multi-purpose rooms close to the digital spaces.


You can do pre-production activities like make-up, script study, meetings, storyboarding, and many more.

MAIN SPACES

DigiPARC is now serving your digital needs for both corporate and private events like meetings, product launches, webinars, live sessions, event performances, parties, and others.


Traditional events that were done physically before can now be conducted here in our innovative studios digitally.
